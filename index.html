Background

Suppose that you want to buy a digital audio player.
You start researching the information but the amount of data put you off.
Then you start wishing for digital audio player version of product chart. This application is created to do the job.

Data

The data of top 15 best selling digital audio player in Japan was collected from kakaku.com on 7 Feburary 2016. The following information were collected.

  • Product name
  • Company
  • Price (yen)
  • Storage (GB)
  • Weight (g)
  • Battery time (hr)
  • Bluetooth

How to use

Head to this shiny application to see the product chart.

Hope you will find the digital audio player of your dream!
